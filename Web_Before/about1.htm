Who We Are

AboveE is a Business Process Management (BPM) service, consulting, technology, and solutions provider. It was established in May 2000 by a team of researchers from AT & T Bell Laboratories. Its mission is to provide the best BPM solutions and the best business process consulting and technical services to assist customers to utilize existing IT investments, optimize business processes,strengthen rapid organizational adaptation to changes, and increase their long term competitiveness and profitability. Our products and services meet world-class standards, and have received numerous international awards.
What We Do

We determine the BPM needs of our customers. Through developing BPM products, processing consulting services, mastering the technical architecture, and constructing a customized BPM, a suitable integrated enterprise class BPM solution is deployed into the customer organization to effectively improve their business process. Many well known companies around the world are using our solution to increase their operation efficiency and competitive advantage.

The Power Process Suite developed by AboveE adheres to WfMC standard component framework. By applying Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and developed 100% by using Java programming language, it has the utmost flexibility, expandability, stability, and portability that can easily support various enterprise applications or integrate into existing IT systems. From integrating various business processes such as internal materials management, marketing channels, accounting management, cost management, capital management, purchasing, material distribution, and office automation. Whether it is an interdepartmental or cross system process, complex form management, external supply chain information sharing, commercial activities among multinational subsidiaries, or B2Bi commerce solution , by utilizing the Power Process BPM Platform, horizontal business processes, documents, and forms can integrated, enabling the management of multiple systems.
 
 

Our Beliefs

We are a world-class BPM software and services provider with the ultimate BPM solutions. We assist our customers to easily and effectively manage and utilize their business processes, enabling customers to optimize their processes to maximize benefits.
