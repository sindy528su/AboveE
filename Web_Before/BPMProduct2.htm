BPM Solution
 
 
 

The founding AboveE team members decided when the company was incorporated to develop the EAI and B2Bi products. First, AboveE was able to comprehend the BPM needs of businesses. As a result, in April 2002, the first main product, Power Process BPM 2.0, was introduced. On October of the same year, the RosettaNet Channel Manager/RosettaNet B2Bi Management System was introduced to assist customers in optimizing intra-organization and inter-organization processes by increasing the overall process communication in the supply chain system.
 

From then on, it invested more research funds to continuously increase the performance and stability of the system. As a result, in May 2005 it introduced Power Process BPM 4.2, which was very competitive and qualified to become the “Core Organizational Competitive Advantage”It assisted business customers to better describe and comprehend existing processes to design even more suitable processes based on the needs of the business to ensure its flexibility and swiftness in adapting to environmental and market changes.
