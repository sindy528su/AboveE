AboveE: Recipient of Rewards Year After Year

Since AboveE was established in May 2000, it has been researching and developing business process platforms. After two years of hard work, it received the “2003 IBM Software Websphere Best Portal Value Added Solutions Award” for excellence in technology and service. After that, it received awards year after year and countless accolades. In particular, it received the Gold Award of the Global Awards for Excellence in Workflow, presented by the Workflow and Reengineering International Association. AboveE was the only Taiwanese BPM company to receive this reward during the 15 year history of this award. It was also the first company in Taiwan to successfully integrate Portal, BPM, ERP, and B2B. Therefore, AboveE has surpassed its local BPM peers and are among the leading multinational vendors.
