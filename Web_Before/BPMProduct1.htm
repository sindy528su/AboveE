BPM Solution
 
 

 

To assist your business in successfully introducing a Business Process Management (BPM) system, we apply years of accumulated experience in the BPM field, introducing leading technology, and a strong team to meet the strategic operating needs of the customers and a series of professional services that includes conducting a comprehensive professional BPM analysis, consultation sessions with consultants, educational training, and technical support to customize the most competitive business process.

 

Out customers tell us that our professional service can effectively lower the risks associated with introducing BPM while receiving the maximum benefit from the introduction of BPM. It also enables every employee to interact more frequently and increase their performance and productivity to better deal with the ever-changing modern competitive landscape.

 
 
Process Case Analysis

POC Design Services

This is the first step in successfully constructing a BPM. The BPM team at AboveE is armed with professional technical knowledge and comprehensive services to guarantee a successful introduction of a BPM system into your company. At this stage, the professional team at AboveE analyzes the current processes and their actual execution to determine areas that can be improved in order to provide suggestions on how they can be gradually improved and establish the necessary processes. Our case analysis can define major processes, confirm processes, determine the relationship among organizations and systems, determine modes of cooperation among internal departments, and clarify the roles of each person involved during the introduction of the BPM project.

AboveE can meet the needs of business by drafting process outlines and the user interface model to enable users to have an actual look and feel. It can further be used as a basis for confirming user requirements.

 
BPM Project Construction

Lower Risks of Introduction

To ensure the smooth operations of the BPM system, data integrity, and correctness of processes, AboveE integrates existing ERP, CRM, PLM, and other systems by utilizing EAI, API, and Web Services. The system is then configured according to the business requirements and technical architecture, such as designing the process and configuration of the organizational structure. We even provide business users to conduct functionality tests, integration tests, and user tests while providing technical instruction, suggestions for improvement, and assistance in resolving execution problems.
To lower the risks of introducing the BPM system, AboveE can integrate existing systems and deploy BPM features in multiple phases according to business needs and user preferences in order to lower the sense of unfamiliarity felt by employees with the operating system. We also provide advance planning to ensure flexibility in adjusting and extending features in the future. We strive to become long term IT partners of businesses.
