The BPM Product Family developed by AboveE provides businesses the best business process management solution, including:

The core software is written 100% in Pure Java and utilizes XML for communications. Other benefits includes high stability, high portability, multiplatform, and cross systems.

 

The system architecture is a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). Building on a Standard Based Open Architecture, it applies the latest Web Service technology to integrate the systems and distribute processes.

 

In terms of software quality management, Object Oriented Methodology and Capacity Maturity Management are applied to analysis, design, programming, and testing, ensuring the quality and expandability of the software product.

 
The management team brings years of practical experience in large scale project information planning from AT&T. Through the guidance of academia and consultants from related areas, our customers can be assured that the most complete consulting support services are being provided.
