BPM Solution
 
 
 

Power Process BPM Standalone Edition is centered on a powerful process engine, with the organizational management, event management, process designer, process monitor, and bridging control panels implemented as separate independent modules that can interact with each other to increase performance. This enables process designers, process managers, and users to each be accordingly assigned roles in the design, process, response, and monitoring of various complex and simple business processes, resulting in close integration of the users and the system. Therefore, this releases corporate activeness and re-enforces organizational competitive advantages.
Modules Features and Properties

Powerful and Stable Process Engine

This is the event-driven core that consisting of process information. The lightweight process engine is powerful enough to process up to a million transactions and used in tens of thousands of complex business processes used by users without losing any stability.
 

Comprehensive Organization Manager

Foreign designed software usually cannot meet the needs of Asian businesses to have highly flexible and diverse organizational management. AboveE, a Taiwanese company with a global view, knows the characteristics of companies operating on both sides of the Taiwan Strait . Regardless of the different types of organizational structures, large numbers of people, large numbers of position types (roles), and flexible substituting mechanisms, can be easily queried and configured based on “Process, Department, Role, and Personnel”. In addition, personnel properties can be divided into three access levels: properties the user can modify itself (such as: address and contact person, and properties users can only view but not modify (such as: rank), and properties cannot be viewed by users (such as: performance). Aside from the build in default user properties, additional properties can be added as needed and can be used to determine personnel assignments in processes.
 

Modeler Supporting Collaborative Operation Business Processes

Graphical visualized process module design enables the designer to conveniently define the process environment, resulting in higher accuracy and more complex interacting processes. By ensuring the logic used complies with the BPMN standard process diagram, the processes can interact with one another. The SubFlow design concept enables resources to be used repeatedly, increasing the flexibility and efficiency of the design process. The collaborative development architecture enables processes to be designed offline by multiple designers, increasing the efficiency of the process design and re-engineering.
 

Exceptions and Time Out Manager

Providing mechanisms for dealing with exceptions and time outs enables processes to continue to function when such evens occur.
 

Dynamic Alert Manager

Process designers can design different alert templates based on different times and roles (such as: 2 hours / instance, 4 hours/instance, or 6 hours/instance) to deliver to different recipients to ensure guaranteed delivery.

 
Event Manager

By applying an object oriented processing method, the internal polling mechanism automatically reacts to transactions or business events, increasing the degree of sensibility and reaction of businesses. The user can sort yet to be processed events based on its urgency to maintain the timeliness and efficiency of processes. A time alarm and automatic detection of low supply quantity can be configured, such as “idle due to waiting for supplies” can result in significant losses to manufacturing line assembling industries. By configuring the system to check for the supply quantity, an alert may be sent to the relevant personnel.
 

Internal Audit Monitor

Through the brand new Web Monitor design concept, all that users need to do is to be connected to the Internet to monitor work processes that can start, hold, stop all processes/sub-processes, and to conduct a deep analysis of the process before troubleshooting problems occur in order to ensure changes to the organization can be resolved online and ensure the accuracy and execution of the decision to meet the ever changing demand of businesses.

 

Powerful Integration Manager

Aside from providing regular API integration method, other multiple components include: Adapter components such as: Database Java Bean, Command line, Com, File Polling, Web services, JMS (Enterprise Edition), and EJB (Enterprise Edition). The Adapter Wizard bridge connection can quickly develop what it needs to modify, stop, and enable the Adapter through the management interface.
