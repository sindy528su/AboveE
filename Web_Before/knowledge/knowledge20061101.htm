Properties of High Quality BPM Systems

When enterprises select BPM systems, depending on the size of the project, the budget can be from hundreds of thousands to tens of millions of dollars. However, the benefits of deploying BPM systems are much greater in the long run than the initial investment amount. Here, we provide properties that good BPM systems should have.

Can support Workflow, Process, Automation, B2B Process, Integration, Ad Hoc Process, and the needs of various processes such as front end sales processes , strict back end administration processes, and processes design by the enterprise at different times for different goals.
Conforms to the WfMC standard components architecture, as well as graphical process design tools that complies with the Gateway logic found in the BPMN standard.
Provides a large diverse choices of forms that can be used according to the needs of the process, provide correct and real time decisions, enabling close relations between people, process, and information.
Provide API, Web Services, Adapters, Rendezvous, Workflow Event Listener and multiple integration methods that can integrate closely with different systems as needed.

Enterprises with global operations have many links, regardless of the Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Mainland China operating, global time zone management, or global inventory allocation models, a good BPMS must provide detailed features to support the organization operating needs.

Not only can handle unit time and work loading employee work efficiency analysis, but can monitor the whole process from start to beginning. Stoppages due to organization changes can be corrected online, to return the best response.
