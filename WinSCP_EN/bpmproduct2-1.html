eFDoc

A new generation of Power Process serves as the core of BPM Power Process BPM Suite, comprising a flow server, a flow designer, an integration manager, a scheduling manager, a work portal, and a BPM manager as functional modules. Although the modules are independent, they can support each other. The process server has a high-level parallel processing capability; flows for both cross-function and inter-system in an enterprise may be stably implemented.
 

Reliable Process Ser ver

The reliable process server with the high-level parallel processing capability provides a complete fault tolerance measure "System Integrity Recovery", which, when the network or database is interrupted, allows the system re-connect, automatically detects and re-start the flow that was interrupted, to meet the needs of large enterprise deployment, high-load environment, and complex back-end platform integration.
 

Nimble Organization Structure Management

Power Process BPM Suite 6.0 provides full-function organization structure management tool with strong integration capability to easily accomplish with multi-nation or complex organizational structures. The embedded auto detection program minimizes error occurrence and allows the system to obtain real-time and accurate organization information through regular conversion of original organizational structure data, such as Active Directory, LDAP or Notes LDAP etc., which effectively solves process maintenance issues when e-organization takes place, which largely reduces the burden of system maintenance.

Reliable Process Engine

Power Process BPM Suite 6.0 provides users with simple and practical standard process modeling design tool; with friendly graphical user interface that helps IT personnel save trivial details and easily set up all the components required on the web page via mouse drag, drop, click and select, as well as accomplish BPMN standard of process design and form production. Under certain circumstances, users will be able to convert the personal or departmental needs to an executable process by their own with no dependence on IT people, allowing users to clearly and completely outline and optimize workflow via diverse angles.
 

Unique Business Rule Management

Most of the organizations have hundreds of business rules and guidelines for workflows and decision-making process. Power Process BPM Suite 6.0 enables an effective and convenient tool that helps manage frequently-changed rules and simplify the process design maintenance, where the definition, execution and maintenance of business rules can be physically realized by managers or operation analysts, allowing enterprises to establish changes faster, safer and more effective.
 

Convenient eForm Setting Widget

Considering the large demand of eForms, we provide step-by-step eForm setting widget. By simply following the widget setting descriptions, allowing tight connection of eForm and data source, moreover, the eForm design is no more a privilege of process developer.

 

Complete Auditing and Overdue Mechanism
In responding to the needs of various tasks handling, Power Process BPM 6.0 provides complete auditing and overdue mechanism, where users will be able to set up common notice through e-mail alert,SMS alert, etc. Multi-national calendar is provided to those multi-national companies for easy time-zone work-hour calculation, authorization and workflow management.


Reliable Process Engine and Safety Control Mechanism

A new-generation Power Process BPM Suite 6.0 provides stable and reliable process engine which caters the needs of deployment within the large-scale enterprises. An access control and management mechanism provides multi-layer ACL (Access Control List) which largely reinforce the overall safety environment.
 

New Dynamics of Process Evolution

A complete business process management capability of Power Process BPM Suite 6.0 helps enterprises construct a smooth, convenient, highly stable process platform to achieve high return on investment.
