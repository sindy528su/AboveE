Human Resource Management Solution
Human resource processes cover wide areas. From recruiting new talent, new hire procedures, asset management, salary management, performance review, education & training, absentee & on-leave arrangements, retirement assessment, termination procedures, and etc.... The human resource management solution from AboveE can effectively connect all departments involved, including inter-country and inter-regional organizations into the process. Through fair and standardized procedures, human source management has become more convenient and automated. A web-based interface human resource platform is especially effective for businesses trying to manage large number of employees that are geographically distributed over a wide area.
 

Business Challenges
Since all human resource process involves individuals from many departments and even other organizations, human resource processes are often nightmares for many companies. For example, preparation needs to be done for new hires to enable them to start working. This includes new hire procedures and new hire profiles (human resource department, new hires need a computer (purchasing department), office space management, new telephones, (management department and hiring department management), computer network & email account (IT department), and any general and professional training. The above arrangements all need to be ready by the time the new hire is on board. Otherwise, new employees may have nothing to do and have nothing to communicate with others, resulting in misconceptions about the company that may result the new hire to quickly leave the company. Or businesses may set up foreign operations, but due to differences and local customs, in some locations, there is higher competition for new talent. By the time corporate headquarters receive the information, the information may already be outdated, thus posing management problems.
 

For activities that involve the whole business such as human resources, if information from each department cannot be connected, then employees from all departments and salary adjustments would result in endless paperwork, creating a heavy workload. This would be a distraction from strategic tasks that are beneficial to the business, resulting in delays, and finally becoming the bottleneck that is preventing the business from growing. These problems can all be solved with process automation
